fun book club is a cross-functional group of friendly folks from all backgrounds who read nerdy books together.

Making time for reading can be hard, but exposing yourself to new ideas and learning from fields that may sit adjacent to your area of expertise is always worth doing.

fun book club is a little group of people who are in (or interested in) the tech industry who keep each other motivated to read and discuss one book a month. We are not limited to any one field of expertise — the idea is to have designers, engineers, product people, test analysts, anybody — all

how it works

  • every month we read a book from our list on Trello (anyone can add suggestions and vote)
  • we have a chat room on Discord if you’d like to stay in touch throughout the month
  • then we get together (online) and have a relaxed guided discussion about the book

our guiding principles

this is a safe place

fun book club is dedicated to being a safe, inclusive, welcoming, and harrassment-free space. We have absolutely zero tolerance for discrimination or bullying of any kind. We are working on a Code of Conduct that will be linked here before we go live. It will be strictly enforced.

leave your ego at the door

This is not a place for grand-standing or one-upping others. Stay humble and remember that you can always learn from others. Try to listen as much as you talk!

embrace the “beginner's mind”

Have an attitude of openness, eagerness, and lack of preconceptions when reading books and approaching discussions, even if it's a topic you're familiar with (or even an expert in).

have fun!

Don't take the fun book club too seriously. It's supposed to be a light and, well, fun little group — don't stress if you fall behind on your reading or if you feel like other folks seem to know more about a particular topic than you do. We're just here to read books and have a chin wag!
